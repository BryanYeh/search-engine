 Serving approximately 20,500 students primarily from the greater Los Angeles area. Cal State L.A. operates year round on the quarter system. Four quarters, each 11 weeks in duration. Cal State L.A. is organized into six colleges that incorporate 50 academic departments and divisions offering a variety of majors. Six colleges offer nationally recognized science, arts, business, criminal justice, engineering, nursing, education and humanities programs at Cal State LA.

 

Golden Eagle Territory

Check your admission status, check and update your personal portfolio (address, holds, to-do lists). You will need a GET User ID in order to log in. You can also log in to GET from the myCSULA Portal using the myCSULA Identity account

Athletics

 

The Golden Eagles are member of the California Collegiate Athletic Association and compete on the Division II level of the NCAA. Visit the CSULA Athletics website for more information.
