Direct TV Line-up

The following represents the updated Direct TV channel line-up that we based on residents' suggestions.

If you experience trouble getting these channels, you may need to reset your channel lineup by using the menu options on your TV. If the problem persists, log onto the Housing Portal and fill out a Work Order.

Please note that some of the channels have an east coast feed. You can get station information from Direct TV by checking out their Channel & Guide webpage. Remember to input 'Zip Code 90032.'
