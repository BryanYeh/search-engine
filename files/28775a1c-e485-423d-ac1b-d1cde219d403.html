Welcome to Neurological Surgery at UCSF


The Department of Neurological Surgery at UCSF is home to leading physicians and scientists dedicated to treating patients with neurosurgical disorders and to the search for better therapies and cures. Our primary commitment is to our patients and we are ranked in the top five of US News & World Report's list of best neurosurgery services in the country — and best on the West Coast. We have neurosurgery services at all major hospitals in the San Francisco Bay Area, providing a full range of neurosurgical subspecialty care.
