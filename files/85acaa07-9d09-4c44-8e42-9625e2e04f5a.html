Housing Community

Housing Services offers affordable and convenient 2 and 4 bedroom apartments located on the Cal State LA campus. Click here to read more about our apartments.

How To Apply ›

The Housing License Agreement serves as your contract for housing. It is available online and requires a payment of $140.

A student application file is considered complete when the Housing Services office has received the Housing Services License Agreement, the Tuberculosis Verification Form, and Medical Consent Form (if under 18 years of age). Make sure you view the Payment Schedules and select the right one for you.

English Language Program & Study in LA Students 

A number of apartments are designated for students enrolled in the English Language Program (ELP) or Study in LA.  Students will be assigned with matriculated CSULA students to promote cross cultural awareness and enhance their learning through practical experience.

International Students

If you are a matriculated International Student with a Campus Identification Number (CIN), please read the Payment Schedules and Payment Plans, and apply online forfor housing.

ADA Singles

Single occupancy rooms may be requested by students with documented disabilities as supported by the Office for Students with Disabilities (OSD). Designated apartments can accommodate students with mobility, visual, hearing impaired and other disabilities. If you require special accommodations, please register with OSD. You may reach their office at (323) 343-3140 or find additional information on their website at www.calstatela.edu/univ/osd.
