Fee Waiver Program

The purpose of the Fee Waiver program is to provide an opportunity for eligible employees to take courses to attain a credential, undergraduate or graduate degree (Career Development) as part of an approved Employee Development Plan and/or courses that will enhance current job skills (Job-Related). Eligible employees may also transfer their fee waiver benefit to a spouse or dependent child.

Job-Related: A job-related course is taken for the purpose of improving the level of skill needed to perform existing duties and responsibilities or acquiring additional skills needed to perform new assigned duties and responsibilities.

Career Development: Career Development is designed for the purpose of enrolling in courses, as part of an approved employee Development Plan.

Graduate Assistants, student assistants and teaching associates are not eligible for fee waivers.
