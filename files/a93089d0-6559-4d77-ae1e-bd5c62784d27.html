ALUMNI ASSOCIATION STANDING COMMITTEES

If you are interested in getting more involved in the Alumni Association with a greater commitment than helping out at an occasional program or event, a standing committee might be right for you.

Standing committees meet regularly (usually 4-6 times a year) to discuss policies and procedures surrounding different areas of the Cal State L.A. Alumni Association programming and business.

Current standing committees include:

Alumni Scholarship Committee

The Alumni Scholarship Program provides students with financial assistance in recognition of their leadership, service and participation in student activities, and academic excellence. Each year the recipients are selected through an application and interview process conducted by the scholarship Committee of alumni volunteers.

The Alumni Scholarship Committee determines policies and procedures for the scholarship selection process, and directs the selection process.

Staff Liaison: Maria Ubago '96 '06 (323) 343-4945, mubago@cslanet.calstatela.edu.

Membership / Communications Committee

The Membership / Communications Committee oversees goal setting and result analysis of the Association's membership acquisition and renewal program.  In addition, it ensures that the Alumni Association is communicating effectively and consistently with alumni by coordinating concise, compelling, and interesting messaging; and increasing the visibility of the Alumni Association within the local community. Members of this committee include the Alumni Network Representatives and alumni who are leaders in the community.

Staff Liaison: Maria Ubago '96 '06 (323) 343-4945, mubago@cslanet.calstatela.edu.

Advocacy Committee

The advocacy committee creates and coordinates support for Cal State L.A. and the California State University among State, Federal and locally elected officials through advocacy efforts of individual alumni, alumni organizations and campus officials. The responsibilities of the committee members are to work with staff in determining program priorities; to assist in planning evaluation programs; to help staff in recruiting, mentoring, and training advocacy program participants; to attend advocacy committee events and play a leadership role in order to become articulate spokespersons on Cal State L.A. and CSU legislative and governmental issues; and to become knowledgeable about legislative advocacy techniques and volunteer political involvement.

Staff Liaison: Maria Ubago '96 '06 (323) 343-4945, mubago@cslanet.calstatela.edu.

Finance Review Committee

The committee is responsible for general oversight and review of Association finances, funds, expenditures, revenue, taxes, and investments.

Staff Liaison: Maria Ubago '96 '06 (323) 343-4945, mubago@cslanet.calstatela.edu.

If you are interested in serving on any of these committees, complete the online volunteer information form (indicating the committees in which you are interested) and you will be contacted promptly with more information.

BOARD OF DIRECTORS

The Board of Directors consists of 23 volunteers who develop central policy and procedure for the Cal State L.A. Alumni Association.

Nominations for board positions are accepted year-round for inclusion in the selection process in spring of each year. Board terms begin on July 1st of each year.

If you are interested in serving on the Board of Directors, please contact Maria Ubago '98 '06, Executive Director of Alumni Relations, at (323) 343-4945 or email mubago@cslanet.calstatela.edu.
