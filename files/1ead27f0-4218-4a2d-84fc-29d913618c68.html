Golden Eagles Apartments

Housing for Upper Division Students


Upper division students can look forward to affordable housing with single occupancy in two-bedroom apartments. There are suite-style and double occupancy rooms for students on a stricter budget. Living at the Golden Eagles Apartments gives you an opportunity to meet and live among upper division students and enhance your academic experience. Take advantage of our quiet community living with convenient access to classes, faculty, libraries, research facilities and other campus resources. It is perfect for students without cars and only minutes away from class.

Meal Plans

Though kitchens are available in your apartment/suite, you can sign up for one of the meal plan options available. Get Meal Plan information, click here.

Criteria for GEA

  • Must be an enrolled student at CSULA with a minimum of 12 undergraduate or 8 graduate units during each quarter (fall, winter and spring). Exceptions may be granted by the Director of Housing Services or a designee.
  • Upper division or graduate student status.
  • University account must be current.
  • Conduct in good standing, including no open, repeat or high level conduct cases (applies to current residents living in Ph. I or II requesting to transfer to GEA)

Amenities

All apartments share a living room, kitchen and restroom. Suites share a kitchen and restroom.

Community Center

Wireless Laundry facilities and a meeting room are available in the Community Center.

Wireless Laundry Facilities
Check on the status of your laundry from the comfort of your own home computer or cell phone by logging into www.washview.com. Check how many minutes you have left on you machine or have an email or text sent to you when your laundry is complete.

Parking

Limited parking is available on-site at the GEA (under and behind bldg 3). Additional CSULA parking is available in Lot 7A and Mariondale Avenue. Street parking around GEA is also available but pay attention to street cleaning day so you can move your car. Click here to get additional parking information.

Evacuation

The evacuation site for GEA is on the grassy median on Mariondale, just south of GEA.

Location, Map and Directions

The Golden Eagles Apartments are located at 5425 Dobbs Street, Los Angeles, CA 90032, on the corner of Mariondale and Valley Blvd. Click here to see a map of GEA. To get directions from the Housing Office to GEA, click here.
