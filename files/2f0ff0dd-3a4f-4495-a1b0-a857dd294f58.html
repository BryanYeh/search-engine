Housing for Orientation Students

Welcome to the Conference/Guest Housing Services program at California State University, Los Angeles. We are pleased to share information about our services with you.  In addition to our apartment-style accommodations that highlight privacy, comfort, and convenience we offer recreational activities and meeting spaces (in Housing and on campus: Golden Eagle Building, University-Student Union).

At Housing Services, we offer a secure and comfortable living environment, combined with other amenities that include furnished 2 and 4 bedroom apartments equipped with central heating and air conditioning. Our guest apartments accommodate 1-200 people during our conference period, last week of June through first week of August.  Space is limited and reservations are made on a first come, first serve basis.  Guests share a fully furnished living room, dining room area and ample storage space in each apartment.  For those who prefer to eat in, our fully equipped kitchens (not including cookware and utensils) include a stove, microwave, refrigerator/s and dishwasher.  We also offer prepared meals for your convenience.  Meal plans are available so check out our dining options and we will make the arrangements for you.

The Phase I Community Center houses the Game Room, Computer Lab, Vending Machine area and the Community Information Center (CIC).  Throughout Phase II, you will find the Housing Services office, The Dining Commons, Conference Room, Lounge, basketball and volleyball court, laundry rooms and additional vending machines. Just a stairway away, you will be dazzled by our brand new University Student Union, equipped with lounges and meeting rooms. Visit our world-class facility, The Luckman Fine Arts Complex and our library on your spare time.

The Housing Services here at California State University of Los Angeles is happy to offer housing to a variety of conferences. This includes both youth and adult groups covering a range of activities including, camps, forums and workshops. The Conference Room is located inside the Phase II Lounge, just a few steps from the Dining Commons.
