Move-Out Information

Prior to moving out, you MUST receive approval by Housing Services to cancel your License Agreement. The Student Housing License Agreement serves as your housing contract and is for a specific occupancy period, which ends at 2:00 pm on June 13, 2015.  Please keep in mind, that moving out (abandoning space) without approval does not release a student from financial responsibility.

Please review the Section III.C.4 under the Terms and Conditions of the Student Housing License Agreement, which outlines the conditions for release that will be considered by Housing Services (reasons and financial responsibility).

  • The agreement states that only those students who are graduating, withdrawing from Cal State L.A., or getting married will be released from their contract with appropriate supporting documentation.
  • All other reasons may be considered with appropriate supporting documentation and with proof of changes in circumstances from the time you completed the License Agreement to now.
  • Review the Terms and Conditions of the Student Housing License Agreement available online at http://web.calstatela.edu/univ/housing/License_Agreement.php
  • Submit the Request for Exception Form to the Housing Services office at least 30 days before the end of the quarter. Click here to print the Request for Exception Form


Approved Winter 2015 Move-Outs
If you are a current resident and have been approved to move out at the end of the Fall 2014 quarter, you must clean your apartment and be completely move-out by 2:00 pm on Saturday, March 21, 2015.

Check-Out PROCEDURES
If you have requested the early termination of your license Agreement and have been approved by Housing Services to move out, you have two check-out options.

Option 1: Check-Out with a RA:
If you want to have a Housing Staff do a walk-through (inspection) of your apartment, you must make a check-out appointment at least one week prior to your planned check-out date.  Choosing this option means you will prepare for your move ahead of time, organize, pack and clean prior to your anticipated check-out date. On your confirmed check-out date, the RA will meet you at your apartment at your scheduled appointment. Prior to their arrival, you must have completely removed all of your belongings and cleaned your apartment. If you choose to check-out with a staff member, you will complete the ACR, note damages, sign and date the form and turn in your keys.

If you do not wish to inspect your apartment with a staff member, you may use Option 2 (below). You must be completely moved out and have returned the key(s) before 2:00 pm or additional charges may be assessed.

Option 2: Check-out using the Express Check-out System:  
You may pick up an Express Check-out packet (instructions, envelope, & Mail Forwarding Form) from the Housing Services Office. Fill out the information on the envelope, sign, date it and place your keys inside and seal it closed. Return this envelope to the Housing Office. After hours, you may deposit the envelope in the designated key drop box under the counter at the Housing Office (Phase II). Using the Express envelope waives your right to contest any damage charges that are attributed to you, including any shared charges with roommates.
