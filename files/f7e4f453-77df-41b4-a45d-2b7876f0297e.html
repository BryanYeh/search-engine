Employment Opportunities

Welcome to California State University, Los Angeles Job Opportunities. Available Openings are updated on a continuous basis. If a position is listed on this site it is currently available.

Due to maintenance, the online job application system may be unavailable on Wednesdays between 10 p.m. to midnight. Please check back at a later time. We apologize for any inconvenience as we improve our system.

A COMPLETED ONLINE CSULA EMPLOYMENT APPLICATION IS REQUIRED. RESUME ATTACHMENTS ARE ENCOURAGED, BUT WILL NOT BE ACCEPTED IN LIEU OF THE APPLICATION.

In addition to meeting fully its obligations under federal and state law, California State University, Los Angeles is committed to creating a community in which a diverse population can live and work in an atmosphere of tolerance, civility and respect for the rights and sensibilities of each individual. To that end, all qualified individuals will receive equal consideration without regard to economic status, race, ethnicity, color, religion, national origin or cultural background, political views, sex or sexual orientation, gender, or other personal characteristics or beliefs.

The University actively encourages qualified minorities, women and persons with a disability to apply. Upon request, reasonable accommodation will be provided to individuals with protected disabilities to (a) complete the employment process and (b) perform essential job functions when this does not cause an undue hardship. We are an Equal Opportunity/Title IX Employer.
