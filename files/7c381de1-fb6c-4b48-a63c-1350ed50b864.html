News & Announcements

Applying for Housing!

The Spring 2015 application is now available online.

Forms to turn in when applying to Housing

  • As a reminder, all residents must submit the Tuberculosis Form (TB) to the Health Center on an annual basis.
  • The Meningitis Form is required from all first-year students moving into Housing. 
  • If you are under the age of 18, a parent or guardian must sign and agree to the terms and conditions of the License Agreement and complete the Consent Authorization Form for Medical Treatment.

Steps to follow when applying to Housing

  • Cal State L.A. and International students, apply here.
  • English Language Program (ELP) and Study in LA students, apply here.
