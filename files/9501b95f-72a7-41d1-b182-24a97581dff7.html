CAREER NETWORK - ongoing

Career Network volunteers agree to be available as an informational network contact for Alumni Association members and students. Volunteers may be current students, faculty, staff, alumni and friends. Career Network volunteers may be asked to talk about current or past jobs, provide general advice, and critique resumes, but are under no obligation to help networkers find specific career opportunities - help is of a more information and general nature.

To inquire about volunteer opportunities, contact the Alumni Association at (323) 343-2586 or email alum@cslanet.calstatela.edu.
 
 

STUDENT SCHOLARSHIP SELECTION

Scholarship volunteers participate in student selection through the reading, scoring and interviewing of scholarship applications. All Alumni Association members are eligible to participate.

To inquire about volunteer opportunities, contact Maria Ubago '98 '06, Executive Director of Alumni Relations, at (323) 343-4945 or email mubago@cslanet.calstatela.edu.
 
 

GRADFAIR '14

GRADFAIR '14 volunteers greet and welcome soon-to-be Cal State L.A. alumni at this exciting "one-stop shopping day" for graduating students.

The Alumni Association will host this exciting event for the 19th year, in April 2014, making it easy for students to complete all of their official and unofficial graduation needs - from confirming eligibility to picking up tickets to purchasing their cap and gown.

To inquire about volunteer opportunities, contact the Alumni Association at (323) 343-2586 or email alum@cslanet.calstatela.edu.
 
 

ALUMNI ADVOCACY COMMITTEE - ongoing

Everyone who lives and works in California has a stake in maintaining a strong and accessible California State University system. That's because the CSU educates 10% of the state's workforce - including a majority of our teachers, computer scientists, engineers and business graduates. With three million newly created jobs expected over the next five years, California's thriving economy is dependent on quality graduates from the world's largest system of public higher education - the CSU.

The Cal State L.A. Alumni Association has formed a special group to help promote public support for CSU through the Alumni Advocacy Committee. Advocacy Committee members volunteer to be campus advocates. Committee members are asked to periodically contact their state legislators when the University's budget is under consideration, new construction is being proposed or support for student and faculty interests is needed. Ambassadors may meet with elected officials when they visit campus. You'll even be kept up-to-date on current happenings in the State Legislature through a special insider's report sent to you four times a year.

Contact Maria Ubago '98 '06, Executive Director of Alumni Relations, at (323) 343-4945 or email mubago@cslanet.calstatela.edu.
